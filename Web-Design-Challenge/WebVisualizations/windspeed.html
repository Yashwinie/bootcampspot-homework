Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude

This visualization showcases the relationship between a city's latitude (i.e. how close it is to the equator) and the windiness of that city (measured in miles per hour).
From this plot, one can see that there could be a trend here -- the visualization seems to suggest that the closer a city is to the equator, the less windy that city was on the day used in the analysis.
